## 3. Problem & Target Audience *Required
**What problem are you solving and who are your target users? (up to 255 characters)**

**Answer:**
Solo travelers struggle to find compatible travel companions, leading to loneliness, safety concerns, and missed experiences. Group travelers face challenges coordinating trips with like-minded people. KOVARI solves this by using AI-powered matching to connect solo travelers and groups based on destination, dates, budget, interests, personality, and lifestyle preferences. Our target users are solo travelers (18-45 years) seeking companions, group travelers looking to expand their travel groups, and adventure seekers wanting to share travel costs and experiences safely.

## 7. D&I Integration *Required
**How has your team considered Diversity & Inclusion (D&I) in your problem definition and solution design? (up to 100 words)**

**Answer:**
KOVARI is designed with inclusivity at its core. Our matching algorithm considers diverse factors including personality types (introvert/ambivert/extrovert), lifestyle preferences, religious compatibility, and language preferences, ensuring users can find compatible companions regardless of background. We support solo travelers of all genders, ages (18+), and travel styles. The platform includes safety features like user reporting, verification systems, and encrypted messaging to protect all users. Our group matching allows diverse groups to form based on shared travel interests rather than demographics. The interface is accessible with responsive design, ARIA labels, and keyboard navigation. We actively prevent discrimination by focusing on travel compatibility over personal characteristics.

**Word Count:** 100/100
